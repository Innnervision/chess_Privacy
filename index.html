📱 Introduction

Chess Kingdom ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Chess Kingdom (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

🔍 Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

Chess Kingdom is designed with privacy in mind. Currently, our app operates entirely offline and does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Device identifiers
  • ❌ User account information
  • ❌ Payment information
  • ❌ Usage analytics
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers
  • ❌ Photos or media files
  • ❌ Contacts
  • ❌ Any other personal data

Information Automatically Collected

The app may access the following device features for functionality purposes only:

  • Storage: Local game data is saved on your device only (game progress, settings, preferences)
  • Network State: To check internet connectivity status (not for data collection)
Important: All data stored is local to your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

💾 How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use, share, or process any personal data.

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game progress and scores
  • User preferences and settings
  • Theme selections
  • Achievement progress

This data:

  • ✅ Remains on your device only
  • ✅ Is never transmitted externally
  • ✅ Can be deleted by clearing the app data
  • ✅ Is not accessible to us or any third parties

🚫 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your information for advertising
  • Transmit your data to external servers
  • Track your activity across apps or websites

👶 Children's Privacy

Chess Kingdom is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages, including children.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

  • ✅ No account creation required
  • ✅ No personal information collected
  • ✅ No online chat or communication features
  • ✅ No behavioral advertising
  • ✅ Safe for children without parental supervision

🔒 Data Security

Even though we do not collect personal data, we take the security of local app data seriously:

  • Local data is stored using Android's secure storage mechanisms
  • Game progress is protected from unauthorized access
  • We follow industry-standard security practices in app development

⚙️ Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

Right to Access

You can view all your game data within the app (scores, progress, settings).

Right to Delete

You can delete all app data by:

  1. Going to Android Settings → Apps → Chess Kingdom → Storage → Clear Data
  2. Uninstalling the app
